The 5-Minute Face

1

Tinted Moisturizer

Dot on forehead, cheeks, nose, chin. Blend with fingers. 60 seconds.

2

Concealer

Under eyes and any blemishes. Tap to blend with ring finger. 45 seconds.

3

Cream Blush

Smile. Dab on apples of cheeks. Blend upward. 30 seconds.

4

Mascara

One coat, upper lashes only. Wiggle from root to tip. 45 seconds.

5

Brow Gel + Lip Tint

Brush brows up. Swipe on lip tint. Done. 60 seconds.
